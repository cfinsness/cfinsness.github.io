Catherine Finsness is a Bermudian filmmaker and artist currently based in San Francisco. She has an MFA in Social Documentary Filmmaking from The School of Visual Arts where she was awarded the competitive Paula Rhodes Award. Her short films have been screened at festivals such as DOC NYC, The San Francisco International Film Festival (SFFilm), Montclair Film Festival, Montreal Independent Film Festival, SVA After School Special, among others. Her experimental films have shown at Bay area venues such as The Roxie and Artist Television Access.

On the side, she continues to work as a marketing operations & technology consultant, helping businesses implement automated campaigns and advanced analytics in CRM platforms. She has worked with many different companies from small start-ups to Fortune 100 companies to help streamline their operations.

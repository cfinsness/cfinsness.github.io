Documentary in Progress

A kaleidoscopic portrait weaving 16mm, digital, 4K, archival videos, and absurdist skits (or "Happenings") in an attempt to capture 95-year-old artist Margaret Fabrizio and her many incarnations: a former harpsichordist who once played with the Grateful Dead; a prolific and early YouTuber; a mother; a quiltmaker; and a lifelong provocateur whose relentless curiosity and experimentation embodies the contradictions and vanishing creative soul of San Francisco

Produced by Catherine Finsness and Poppy Lynch
